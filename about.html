Our Story

Founded in 2010, KHANS TRADING LTD has grown to become one of Mombasa's most reputable car dealerships. We specialize in importing high-quality used vehicles from Japan and Europe, offering our customers the best value for their money.

Our showroom on Haile Selassie Avenue has become a landmark for car buyers in the coastal region, known for our transparent dealings and exceptional customer service.
